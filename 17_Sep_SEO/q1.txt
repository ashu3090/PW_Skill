SEO stands for search engine optimization—the practice of increasing the quantity and quality of traffic to your website through organic search engine results.
The ultimate goal of SEO for business is to generate organic traffic—searchers clicking through to your website from a search engine results page (SERP).
Importance of SEO:
The importance of SEO for business, begins with making sure your site is discoverable by search engines. An SEO-optimized page takes both users and search engines into 
account to drive organic traffic by providing relevant content that meets the needs of those searching.
